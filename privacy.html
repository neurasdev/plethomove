Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

PlethoMove is designed to minimize data collection. PlethoMove does not collect, store, or sell personal information.

Local Transfers

Files transferred with PlethoMove move directly between your devices on your local network. Files are not uploaded to external servers.

Photos and Local Network

PlethoMove may request access to your Photos library and local network only to let you select media, discover nearby devices, and complete transfers you initiate.

Analytics and Tracking

PlethoMove does not use advertising SDKs, third-party tracking, or analytics that profile you across apps or websites.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@plethomove.app.
