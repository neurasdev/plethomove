PlethoMove — Large File Transfer

PlethoMove is designed to move large amounts of photos and videos between your iPhone and Mac with confidence.

Instead of guessing whether a transfer will succeed, PlethoMove gives you clear visibility and control before and during every transfer. From file selection to completion, the app shows important information so you know exactly what to expect.

Whether you're transferring hundreds of photos, large videos, or entire media collections, PlethoMove is built to handle large over-the-air transfers reliably.

Built for Large Transfers

PlethoMove is optimized for transferring large numbers of files at once.

Select many photos and videos in one operation and send them directly to your Mac. PlethoMove is designed for long transfers so you can start the process and let it run reliably without constant supervision.

This makes PlethoMove ideal for:

  • Backing up your entire iPhone photo library to your Mac
  • Moving large batches of photos and videos
  • Transferring media collections between devices
  • Handling large over-the-air file transfers with confidence

Visibility Before You Start

PlethoMove gives you important information while you are selecting files, before the transfer even begins.

During selection you can see:

  • Estimated export time
  • Estimated transfer time
  • Total selected file size
  • Available storage space on your device

This helps you understand the scale of the transfer ahead of time and make informed decisions before starting. This feature allows users to know how much time future transfer might be so user can decide how many files to be selected.

Smart Storage Protection

When files are prepared for transfer, the system may temporarily create copies on the sending device before the files are transmitted.

If your iPhone storage is nearly full, selecting too many files can cause transfers to fail during the transferring.

PlethoMove protects against this situation by checking the available storage on the sending device during file selection.

If the selected files exceed the available temporary space, PlethoMove alerts you immediately so you can adjust your selection before starting the transfer.

This feature is especially useful when your iPhone storage is almost full — which is often when users most need to back up their photos and videos to a Mac.

Remaining Time Estimates

Once a transfer begins, PlethoMove continues to provide clear progress information.

During transfer you can see:

  • Transfer progress
  • Remaining transfer time estimate
  • Clear status updates

This provides peace of mind during large transfers so you always know how the process is progressing.

Set It and Let It Run

Large transfers can take time. PlethoMove is designed so you can start a transfer and let it run reliably.

You can select large collections of photos and videos, begin the transfer, and allow PlethoMove to handle the rest while keeping you informed along the way.

Direct Device-to-Device Transfer

PlethoMove transfers files directly between your devices over your local network.

Your files are never uploaded to external servers.

This means:

  • Faster transfers
  • No cloud storage required
  • Your files stay private

iPhone ↔ Mac Transfers

PlethoMove works both ways:

  • Send photos and videos from iPhone to Mac
  • Send files from Mac to iPhone
  • Optionally save received media directly to the Photos library on iPhone

Requirements

  • Both devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network
  • Wi-Fi and Bluetooth must be enabled

PlethoMove focuses on delivering reliable large file transfers with clear visibility and control, giving you confidence when moving important files between your Apple devices.

PlethoMove(for iOS) is for installation on iPhone. PlethoMove(for MacOS) is for installation on Macbook.
